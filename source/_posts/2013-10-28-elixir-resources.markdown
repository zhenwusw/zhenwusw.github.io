[Elixir](http://elixir-lang.org/) is a functional, meta-programming aware
language built on top of the Erlang VM. It is a dynamic language with flexible
syntax and macrosupport that leverages Erlang's abilities to build concurrent, 
distributed and fault-tolerant applications with hot code upgrades. Joe Armstrong,
one of the inventors of Erlang, even [wrote](http://joearms.github.io/2013/05/31/a-week-with-elixir.html) nice things about Elixir.
