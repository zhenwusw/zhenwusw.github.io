Compilation in Ruby

Jan 18th, 2014

You may think of Ruby as a dynamic scripting language, but, in fact, it uses a compiler just like C, Java, and myan other programming laungages. Rubys’ compiler works b iterating through the AST produced by the tokenizing and parsing processes, generating a series of bytescode instructions along the way.
